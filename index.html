About Quiet Corners of Australia

A Quiet Place to Stay.
A Better Place to Be.

Quiet Corners of Australia is a fully furnished 5-bedroom shared residence at 46 Rodwell Road, Woodchester SA 5255 — a serene 3-acre creekside property surrounded by natural Australian bush.

Designed with healthcare workers in mind, we offer a calm place to rest after long shifts. Reduce commuting fatigue, enjoy ready-to-move-in comfort, and return to a home that supports your wellbeing.

  • 5 fully furnished private bedrooms
  • Peaceful 3-acre creekside property
  • Ideal for nurses after long shifts
  • Short and long-stay flexibility
Conveniently located near
10 mins Strathalbyn
20 mins Mount Barker
25 mins Murray Bridge
